Report: Covid-19 Campaign

By:

Covid-19 has been difficult for every being on the planet. The lucky members of the society have a roof over their head, money and means to buy food and live peacefully in the safety of their homes, but there are people out there who aren’t as privileged and blessed. There are people who have no homes to stay inside and try to stay safe. Team Caritas decided to help these people out as much as they could in their first Covid-19 campaign. 2 slums in North Kolkata were targeted by the team to provide them with some basic requirements to make the pandemic a little easier on them. 100 food kits containing 1kg rice, 250g lentils and some cookies were distributed among the people along with N95 face masks. Aside from these kits and masks, more kits and face masks were sent to an ashram in Salt Lake. The team kept track of everyone’s safety and kept in mind the rules of social distancing and proper sanitation. The members of the team who distributed the things made sure to wear N95 face masks, face shields, gloves and body suits to cover every inch of their bodies and maintain proper sanitation. Markings were made at a distance of 6 feet for people to maintain proper distance, obeying the rules of social distance. A proper queue of only 10 people at a time was maintained to make the distribution safer and easier. The whole system was very systematic and properly maintained. Apart from giving the people food and masks, the team also made them aware about Covid-19 guidelines. They were told about the risks and danger surrounding Covid. They were also enlightened about the importance of social distancing, proper hygiene and sanitation and regular hand wash. They were also advised as to how they could keep themselves out of danger even in an environment as oversaturated as theirs. They were also told about the proceedings they could follow if they were infected by the virus. This initiative by Caritas did not only put a smile on the faces of the people the kits were distributed to but also gave the members of Caritas immense satisfaction of helping the people in whatever way they could. The team was also filled with sadness upon seeing the ways of these people. Caritas hopes to keep helping the people in need as much as they can.
