What is Caritas?

The Government of India announced a nationwide lockdown on the 25th of March 2020, which turned our lives upside down. We keep on complaining about how it ruined all our plans and compelled us to stay inside our own houses. But on how many instances have we stopped bickering about our own problems and thought about those in a condition much much worse than us? How many times have we thought about the thousands of people who lost their jobs, or who are not being able to arrange for food because they were daily wage workers, or the people unable to go back home to their loved ones, or homeless people who don't even have a home to go to?

We need to stop sympathising and start empathising with people. Everyone should try and do their bit to help others, even if it is just one person.

But this situation has put up one more hurdle, and the question that needs to be asked now is, how do we give food or other basic amenities to the ones in need without compromising our safety? Well we at Caritas help solve that problem. We provide those in need with basic requirements like food and sanitizers while ensuring the safety of both, us at Caritas and them.

Caritas was started to provide the people in need get basic amenities like food or sanitation during this Covid pandemic.

Caritas wants to build a society where people are ready to help out the ones who are in for need. Daily wage workers, women & children in slums who cannot manage to even procure their fair share of the smallest of needs.
~Lack of food
~Lack of sanitary products (Sanitary Pads, Masks, Sanitizers )
~Caritas aims to solve that problem , we wish provide those in need with basic requirements like food and sanitizers while ensuring the safety of both, us at Caritas and them.

Moreover , Caritas aims to generate awareness among the urban population. We aim to make them aware that all of us are exactly the same human and that it is the duty of a fellow human to help their friends when in the need for it.
We aim to provide basic requirements like food and sanitation to the masses. We hope to achieve it by your help and support.
